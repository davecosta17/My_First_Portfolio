About Me

Hi, I’m David Asamani DaCosta—a passionate and detail-oriented front-end developer on a mission to craft clean, functional, and visually engaging web experiences. I thrive on turning ideas into interactive realities using nothing but HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

  • Strong HTML Foundations: I build semantic, accessible, and W3C-compliant web pages from scratch. From basic structure to embedded media and forms, I know how to make HTML sing.

  • Creative UI Design: I don’t just code—I design. My pages are thoughtfully structured with intuitive navigation, engaging layouts, and a touch of flair.

  • Project-Based Learning: My GitHub speaks for itself. I’ve built clocks, audio players, solar system simulations, and even a receipt generator—all showcasing my growing mastery of front-end tools.

  • Portfolio Power: I’ve created a personal portfolio that reflects not just my skills, but my story. It includes sections for projects, contact info, and a clean navigation system—all built with semantic HTML5.

  • Collaborative Spirit: Whether it’s embedding tweets or organizing learner data in tables, I know how to make content dynamic and user-friendly.
